About Us

CompliSense-AI is a compliance support platform for EU AI Act alignment. We help organizations assess their AI systems against selected provisions of the regulation through automated scans, remediation guidance, and audit-ready reports.

The platform does not provide legal advice. It assists with technical alignment based on the artifacts you supply—risk registers, model cards, dataset documentation, and more.
