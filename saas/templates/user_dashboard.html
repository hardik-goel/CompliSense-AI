Local Compliance Agent

Download your customized agent to scan systems locally against the regulatory pack you selected.

The local agent scans your codebase and documentation on-prem. By default, only high-level scan metadata (scan ID, timestamps, aggregate scores and counts) is sent back to the SaaS service to power dashboards and history views.
Privacy & Scope

All compliance scanning happens locally on your infrastructure. The SaaS dashboard only receives aggregated metadata (scan IDs, timestamps, article-level scores) and never ingests training data, model weights, or raw logs.

Quick Setup
